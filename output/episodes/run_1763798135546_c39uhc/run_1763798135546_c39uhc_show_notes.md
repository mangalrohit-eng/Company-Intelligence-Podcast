# Show Notes

## Thesis
Sustainability in the Digital Age

## Key Points
- **The Data Dilemma: Navigating the Future of Sustainability**: Unveiling the startling projection of a 165% increase in electricity consumption by global data centers by 2030.
- **Accenture’s Green Revolution**: An overview of Accenture’s sustainability mission in the face of escalating global data consumption.
- **The Industry Responds: Competitors on the Move**: Comparative analysis of how other tech giants are addressing the sustainability challenge in the digital realm.
- **Special Focus: The Power and Peril of Global Data Consumption**: Exploring the broader implications of the projected surge in electricity consumption by data centers on the global environment.
- **Navigating the Future: Key Takeaways**: The imperative for immediate and innovative solutions to address the sustainability challenges posed by global data consumption.
